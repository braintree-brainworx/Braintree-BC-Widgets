# Braintree BC Widgets

Welcome to the documentation for **Braintree BigCommerce Widgets** — a collection of payment widgets that integrate Braintree's payment processing with BigCommerce storefronts.

## What's Included

- **Credit Card Widget** – Hosted fields integration for secure card collection
- **PayPal Widget** – PayPal One Touch and PayPal Credit support
- **Google Pay Widget** – Native Google Pay button integration
- **Apple Pay Widget** – Safari-native Apple Pay button integration
- **Venmo Widget** – Venmo payment support for eligible customers

## Quick Links

- [Getting Started](docs/getting-started) – Installation and initial setup

## Requirements

- BigCommerce store with Stencil theme
- Braintree merchant account
- Node.js 16+ (for local development)
